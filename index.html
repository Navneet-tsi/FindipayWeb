KEY FEATURES

SEND MONEY ANYTIME, ANYWHERE

Support your family whenever, wherever whether in India or Nepal! Enjoy a hassle- free experience when sending money through FindiPay with our Domestic Money Transfer or Indo-Nepal Transfer services.


EASY CASH WITHDRAWAL WITH AePS

Need cash? Or need to check your bank balance? Never run out of cash wherever you are! Making cash withdrawals or checking your bank balance is easy with our AePS service available at our FindiPay stores.


STAY ON TOP OF YOUR BILLS

Say bye bye to late bill payments! Enjoy the convenience of paying all your utility bills on time with FindiPay. No more stress of last date, long queues, and late fines when paying your bills through FindiPay with our Bharat Bill Pay Services.


ENJOY EASY TRAVEL BOOKINGS

Need flight tickets to Goa? Check! Need train tickets to Shimla? Check! Hotel? Check! FindiPay brings you all your travel needs under one roof. Offer hassle free and easy travel bookings through FindiPay with our Flight, Train and Hotel booking services.


APPLY FOR A PANCARD

A PAN card is important to comply with Indian regulatory standards and for all financial requirements. Through FindiPay you can avail PAN card services by following some quick and easy steps!

EASY INSURANCE PREMIUM PAYMENTS

Pay your insurance premiums in just a few clicks with FindiPay. Offer the convenience & ease of paying insurance premiums in a few quick easy steps. No more stress of last date and late fines when paying your premiums through FindiPay with our Premium Payment Services.
